MEMORANDUM

TO: All Staff
FROM: Human Resources
DATE: 2023-06-15
RE: Remote Work Effective Immediately

Effective immediately, the following remote work policy updates apply:

1. ALL employees are now eligible for fully remote work arrangements.
2. Managers must approve remote work requests; default is now FULLY REMOTE unless role requires on-site presence.
3. Core hours are 10 AM - 3 PM in local timezone, with flexibility for meetings outside these hours.
4. Office presence is optional except for mandatory all-hands meetings (quarterly).
5. Equipment: company will provide hardware for all remote workers.
6. No restrictions on working from home vs. other locations (coworking spaces, cafes, etc.).
7. Data security: encrypted VPN required for all connections; personal device use approved with multi-factor authentication.
8. Monitoring policies relaxed to focus on outcomes rather than presence.

This memo supersedes the Remote Work Policy v2.0 for immediate implementation.

Questions? Contact HR at remote-policy@company.com
