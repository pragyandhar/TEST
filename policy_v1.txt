Remote Work Policy v1.0 (2022-01-15)

1. POLICY OVERVIEW
This policy defines guidelines for remote work arrangements within our organization.

2. ELIGIBILITY
Remote work is available to full-time employees who have completed at least 6 months with the company.
Managers have discretion to approve or deny remote work requests based on role requirements.

3. REQUIREMENTS
- Employees must maintain consistent availability during core business hours (9 AM - 5 PM).
- A reliable internet connection is mandatory.
- A dedicated workspace is strongly recommended.

4. SCHEDULE
Remote work may be conducted on a case-by-case basis. Permission must be obtained from management.
Default expectation: employees work from office 5 days per week.

5. RESTRICTIONS
- Confidential documents must not be stored on personal devices.
- All company communications must use approved channels.
- Remote workers are subject to standard monitoring policies.

6. REVIEW
This policy will be reviewed annually. Violations may result in disciplinary action.
