Remote Work Policy v2.0 (2023-03-10)

1. POLICY OVERVIEW
Updated guidelines for flexible work arrangements supporting hybrid and fully remote roles.

2. ELIGIBILITY
Remote work is now available to all full-time and part-time employees.
No minimum tenure requirement applies to new hires in remote-eligible roles.
Managers may approve up to 3 days per week remote work for eligible positions.

3. REQUIREMENTS
- Employees must maintain availability during core business hours (10 AM - 4 PM, flexible).
- A reliable internet connection is mandatory.
- A dedicated workspace is required.

4. SCHEDULE
Remote work schedule is negotiated between employee and manager.
Default expectation: hybrid arrangement (2-3 days remote, 2-3 days office).
Fully remote positions are available for select roles.

5. BENEFITS
- Improved work-life balance.
- Reduced commute time.
- Enhanced productivity for focused work.

6. RESTRICTIONS (UNCHANGED)
- Confidential documents must not be stored on personal devices.
- All company communications must use approved channels.
- Remote workers are subject to standard monitoring policies.

7. REVIEW
This policy will be reviewed annually. Violations may result in disciplinary action.
